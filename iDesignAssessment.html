Welcome to Lesson 01 of Global Citizenship

In this lesson, we will discuss the importance of participation in global citizenship and the kinds of knowledge, skills, and values an engaged global citizen needs to meet global challenges.

We will begin to think about how global citizens find common ground with members of other cultural communities.

Rationale

Engaged global citizens need to be aware of the kinds of knowledge, skills, and values that are required to meet global challenges, including how to find common ground with members of other cultural communities.

الأساس المنطقي

يحتاج المواطنون العالميون المُنخرطون إلى أن يكونوا على دراية بأنواع المعرفة والمهارات والقيم اللازمة لمواجهة التحديات
<العالمية، بما في ذلك كيفية التوصل إلى أرضية مشتركة مع أفراد المجتمعات الثقافية الأخرى.

Objectives

  • Be introduced to the course syllabus and planned activities
  • Define global citizenship, and identify key differences and similarities between global
    citizenship and national citizenship
  • Explore how common ground creates a foundation for building cross-cultural understanding
  • Discuss final project expectations
